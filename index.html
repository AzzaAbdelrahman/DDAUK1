About Our Organisation

The Darfur Diaspora Association is deeply rooted in the lived experiences of its members. Most of our staff are genocide survivors who have endured unimaginable hardship; their voices carry the weight of resilience and the long journey from struggle to progress.

We are more than a charity — we are a community of survivors helping others rebuild their lives. Our unique perspective allows us to advocate for justice, offer culturally sensitive support, and foster trust with those who often feel voiceless.

Founded by members of the Darfuri community in the UK, our association works tirelessly to address the challenges faced by our people both in Sudan and in the diaspora.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A world where justice prevails for the people of Sudan, and Sudanese refugees are empowered and able to live with dignity, opportunity, and hope in a supportive society.

Our Mission

To advocate for justice and equality for victims of genocide, while empowering Sudanese asylum seekers and refugees to rebuild their lives in the UK.

Our Values
  • Justice and equality for all
  • Community empowerment
  • Cultural preservation
  • Compassion and solidarity
  • Transparency & accountability

Our Values

  • Integrity – We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions.
  • Trust – We build and maintain trust through consistency and respect.
  • Empowerment – We uplift individuals and communities to take control of their futures.
  • Neutrality – We remain impartial and focused on human rights.
  • Transparency – We are open and accountable in decision-making.
  • Compassion – We approach all people with empathy and justice.
